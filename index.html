About

The AI Incident Database is the closest thing we have to an institutional memory for AI harm — a public archive that catalogues real-world failures of deployed systems.

Incident Lens turns that archive into something you can see. Three linked visualizations let you trace how reporting has accelerated, which organizations keep showing up, and which populations bear the cost when systems break.
